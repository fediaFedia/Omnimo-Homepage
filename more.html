How does it work?

Omnimo is powered by Rainmeter, a super fast and simple customization tool that works on Windows.
It’s compatible with every other Rainmeter suite.

Best of both worlds


Omnimo simply allows you to place modules and items on your desktop, it doesn’t change the way you use Windows. The startmenu, the taskbar, and even the desktop icons (optional) are still there and integrate really well. Almost like Sidebar Gadgets, you can toggle the modules on & off. It integrates brilliantly into Windows XP, Vista, 7 or 8 and even 10!
